Our Heritage

Established as a cornerstone of excellence, our University Library is proudly recognized as the No. 1 Library in Asia. For over a century, we have served as a sanctuary for scholars, researchers, and dreamers alike.

Our vast archives house over 100,000 physical volumes, ranging from ancient manuscripts to modern scientific journals. Whether you are seeking the quiet rustle of pages in our grand reading hall or exploring our cutting-edge digital repository, we provide an unparalleled environment for intellectual growth.

At the heart of our campus, this library remains more than just a building—it is a living testament to the pursuit of truth and the power of the written word.
